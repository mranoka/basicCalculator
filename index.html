How Much?

We believe that money is but a tool and has no intrinsic value. We therefore recommend that you do not worry about how much it will all cost. Rather, the right question to ask is this: am I ready for a life-cchanging experience?

What do I need to Know?

We believe that the best experiences inlolve one stepping into the unknown. Therefore, we do not reveal the itinerary until the day of the festival. We hope to give you a healthy mix of curiosity, wonder and anticipation
